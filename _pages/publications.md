`AI & Human Decision-Making`

**(1) The Impact of Generative AI on User Behavior in Q&A Platforms: A Focus on Utilitarian Information Seeking**

* **Haewon Sim** and Namil Kim
* Published in *Information Systems Review*, Vol. 27, No. 4, November 2025
* DOI: [10.14329/isr.2025.27.4.443](https://doi.org/10.14329/isr.2025.27.4.443)

This study empirically investigates how user questioning behavior on Q&A platforms has changed following the emergence of ChatGPT. Focusing on the IT/Tech category of Naver Knowledge iN, we find a statistically significant decline in both the number of utilitarian keyword questions and the ratio of questions to searches after generative AI adoption. Based on algorithm aversion theory, findings suggest that user acceptance of AI is contingent on the characteristics of information.

**Keywords:** Human-AI Interaction, ChatGPT, Generative AI, Q&A Platform, Algorithm Aversion
